Document Content Similarity Checker

During my time at the Writing Center, there were several occasions when students upload multiple similar documents for a writing consultant to give feedback on. It was my job to verify which document the consultant should work on. New to Microsoft Word's document comparison tool at that time, I thought it would be useful to create a program that gives me quick numerical confirmations about whether two documents are exact replicas.
