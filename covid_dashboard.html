COVID Impact and Vaccination Efforts Worldwide

Introduction: To gain more experience in creating Tableau dashboards post-graduation, I followed AlexTheAnalyst's video walkthrough on creating a basic dashboard using publicly available COVID data (from Our World in Data).

Inspired by the project, I decided to do a deeper analysis on how COVID impacts different countries, continents, and income classes and present it in a Tableau story. Interactive elements were added to the Tableau story for users to compare how other countries are affected compared to U.S (where I reside) and compare countrywide vaccination efforts with the percentage of population infected in a country.
