Using data visualization best practices, the following charts were created for the purpose of:
1) Enhancing reader comprehension and data storytelling in Explainer articles that aim to convey data limitations considered during the project, and
2) Exploring data from multiple angles to uncover insights, demonstrate project/data potential, and communicate findings to stakeholders.

Analyzing COVID's Worldwide Impact (As of June 2022)

Using COVID-related data from Our World in Data, I analyzed the impact of COVID (death count, and infection and fatality rate) on different economic class, countries, and continents worldwide. To take it one step further, I also included charts on the vaccination progress of different countries for comparison with impact to allow users to see if vaccination was effective in reducing COVID impact.

Tools: Tableau, Microsoft SQL Server, Excel
Reference: AlexTheAnalyst Portfolio Series on YouTube
